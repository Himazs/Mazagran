About Mazagran

Dunedin's Artisan Espresso Bar

Tucked away at 36 Moray Place, Mazagran Cafe is a sanctuary for true coffee lovers. We are absolutely dedicated to the craft — which is why we roast our own coffee right on-site. Whether you're craving one of our consistently smooth signature blends or an adventurous single-origin pour, our expert baristas ensure every cup is flawless. Pair your brew with a treat from our beautifully curated selection of moist cakes, rich tarts, and comforting slices.

What We Offer

Short Black

Smooth, rich, and carefully balanced in-house for a comforting cup every time.

Long Black

Espresso poured over hot water, creating a smooth profile with a full-bodied flavor.

Flat White

A velvety espresso drink topped with silky steamed milk and a thin layer of microfoam.

Cappuccino

A classic coffee crafted with espresso, structured steamed milk, and airy foam dusting.

Latte

A creamy, foundational espresso beverage carefully blended with plenty of hot steamed milk.

Mochaccino

A decadent combination of rich cocoa espresso and textured velvety steamed milk.

Hot Chocolate

A sweet, traditional rich chocolate elixir combined thoroughly with hot steamed milk.

Herbal Tea

A soothing caffeine-free infusion prepared carefully from fresh herbs, flowers, or spices.
